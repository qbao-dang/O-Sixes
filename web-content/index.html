Escape solo queue

Let's face it: solo queue can be rough times. Every match is essentially a pick-up game and you never know who you will get on your team. Your SR is your own, and no one else's. This creates a system where individual play can overshadow team play. O-Sixes solves this problem by putting you on a team that you will play with week-after-week.

Find out more by diving in to see what O-Sixes is all about.

Learn to play as a team

If you want to get better at kicking a soccer ball, you can practice in your back yard. If you want to get better at playing soccer, you need to join a soccer team. The same idea applies to Overwatch. The best way for you to become better is to play on a regular team.

Find a team on O-Sixes or create your own with your friends.

Win as a team

When you have the opportunity to repeatedly play with a set team in a set league, you will learn the strengths and weakness of your teammates. This allows you and your team to grow together and develop strategies that work.

Join a team and start winning as a team.

Be part of a community

Leagues create the environment for teams to compete in. Your team will be set in a division within a league that plays at a set time every week. Remember your loss to Team B two weeks ago? Now you have a chance to play them again next week and pull your team into third place.

Be part of a community of teams competing to be the best in their leagues.
