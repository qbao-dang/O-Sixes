Getting started

Now that you've signed in with your Battle.net account, you are ready to join a league.

Signing up with a League

O-Sixes organizes a league based on time slots. Simply go to the Leagues page and select a league based on when you are available for regular games.

During sign-up, you will provided with two options:

Sign up as an Individual
Based on your role preference and skill level, you will be sorted on to a team.
Sign up as a Team
You will be treated as a team captain. During sign-up, you will have the opportunity to add friends through a Battle.net search. If your friends are not signed up with O-Sixes yet, don't worry! They will automatically receive an invite in their email and they can sign up from there.

What it means to be part of a League

As a part of a league, you will have a game scheduled once a week in the prescribed time slot. You and your team will log onto Overwatch. One pre-determined team captain will be responsible for setting up a Custom Match that everyone will join.
